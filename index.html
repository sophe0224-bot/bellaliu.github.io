纪录片拍摄 · 2025年8月Documentary fieldwork · August 2025

可可西里纪录片Kekexili Documentary

环境叙事、文化记忆和视觉技术。Environmental storytelling, cultural memory, and visual technology.

在可可西里，我用纪录片拍摄理解景观、劳动和生态保护如何成为公共记忆的一部分。这个项目让我意识到，媒体不只是记录现实的方式，也是一种塑造人们理解环境责任的技术。In Kekexili, I used documentary filming to understand how landscape, labor, and ecological protection become part of public memory. This project helped me see media not only as a way to record reality, but as a technology for shaping how people understand environmental responsibility.

支教服务 · 2025年6月Teaching service · June 2025

斯里兰卡融合教育服务Sri Lanka Inclusive Education Service

沟通、无障碍和以人为中心的学习。Communication, accessibility, and human-centered learning.

和斯里兰卡残障儿童一起工作让我明白，沟通并不只靠语言。艺术、动作、手势和游戏都可以成为跨越语言、能力和文化的桥梁，也让我开始关注 AI 如何支持包容教育：帮助表达，但不替代人的关怀。Working with children with disabilities in Sri Lanka taught me that communication is not only verbal. Art, movement, gesture, and play can become bridges across language, ability, and culture. This experience shaped my interest in AI for inclusive education: tools that support expression without replacing human care.

在探路者调研中，我研究 GORE-TEX 材料和智能产品功能等技术优势如何被转化为消费者能理解的价值。我开始关注技术能力和用户实际感知之间的差距。During my Pathfinder research, I studied how technical strengths such as GORE-TEX materials and smart product features are translated into consumer understanding. AI 文化分析工具 · 消费趋势AI culture analysis tool · Consumer trends

反思报告原型Reflection Report Prototype

AI 和数字平台如何帮助年轻人理解线上消费趋势背后的文化力量？How do AI and digital platforms help young people understand the cultural forces behind online consumer trends?

这个原型从 Labubu 等线上消费趋势出发，把“想买”转化为文化问题：身份、社群、稀缺性、博主影响、审美偏好和情绪安慰如何共同塑造欲望？Starting from trends like Labubu, this prototype reframes a shopping impulse as a cultural question: what roles do identity, community, scarcity, influencer effect, aesthetic preference, and emotional comfort play in desire?

这个工具不是为了刺激消费，而是帮助用户暂停、反思，并理解数字平台如何塑造欲望。This tool is not designed to increase consumption. It helps users pause, reflect, and understand how digital platforms shape desire.
